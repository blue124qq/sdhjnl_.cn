iDEA aims to support people from all backgrounds to develop digital, enterprise and employability skills. iDEA enables participants to win 'badges' which can be digital, online modules or events and experiences such as workshops or training sessions.

iDEA badges are awarded for digital and enterprise skills. Each badge carries a number of points which contribute to the iDEA Bronze Award, iDEA Silver Award or iDEA Gold Award.

The iDEA Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions and Cookies Policy have been revised in August 2020 to be compliant with the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation). You can find out your rights regarding how your data is collected, stored and processed in our Privacy Policy. Please use the 'Contact Us' button on the Website if you have any questions or concerns.

Glossary of Terms

  • "Badges": means individual iDEA badges that relate to a specific digital or enterprise skill – these can be online learning modules or events, workshops, experiences or evidence of participation in learning.
  • "Badge Store": means the section on the iDEA platform which contains all the badges available on iDEA.
  • "Completed Badges": means the section in the Badge Store which contains a visual acknowledgement of an individual's achievements showing all the badges they have won. This is the place where Participants can download, print or share their achievements.
  • "Content": means any Content or material, whether of an audio or visual nature and including, but not limited to, words, pictures, music and lyrics, blogs, comments and stories, posted on, by or via the Website.
  • "iDEA": means Inspiring Digital Enterprise Award CIC
  • "iDEA Pioneer": means an institution which has been awarded iDEA Pioneer status to recognise its high levels of engagement with iDEA and shared values with iDEA.
  • "Intellectual Property": means all intellectual property rights including, without limitation, patents, trademarks, registered designs, copyright, design rights, database rights, rights in and to confidential information and know- how, and any rights to apply for the same.
  • "Participant": means anyone who registers on the site to take part in iDEA badges.
  • "Partner": means an organisation of any size that is partnering with iDEA in some way.
  • "Organiser": means anyone who organisers cohorts of learners such as teachers, community leaders, tutors, group leaders, facilitators or team leaders.
  • “Organisation”: means any institution, group, company or establishment which has learners using iDEA or is partnering with iDEA in some way.
  • “Team Member”: means an iDEA user linked to an Organisation who is given access to the Organisation’s iDEA learner analytics by the Organiser.
  • “Organiser Management System”: means the system on iDEA which enables Organisers and Team Members to administer analytics of their cohorts of learners.
  • "Record of Achievement': means the record in PDF format that iDEA creates for any learner who would like to download, print or share their achievements on the platform.
  • "Verification Number": means the unique ID that is generated for you by iDEA and which Participants can give future employers or educational institutions so that they may verify the qualifications a Participant says they have gained on iDEA are correct. This validates and verifies a Participant's accomplishments.
  • "Website": means the iDEA Website, including the badges and any related Website material or Websites.
